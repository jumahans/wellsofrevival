Our History & Mission

The wells of revival Ministry came into being as as result of Gode speaking to Rev. John Ayisi and his wife Pastor Joyce Ayisi. The first sunday service was held on 24th Nov 2001.

The Ministry was born out of a call of god uponHis servant to raise upmen and women of integrity & character who will be a beginning of a new generation wherever they are.

Overturning the purpose of the enemy through spiritual spiritual warfare, changing the history and destiny of their people and becoming an instrument of strength and encouragementto God's people throught strategic praying and declaration of the word in the name of jesusChrist of Nazareth.

This becomes the hub of God's will and purpose for the ministry. A clear vision was realized, recognizing that the primary mandate for the mission of the church was to evangelize, teach and train God called workers.

the driving force behind the siccessof the wells of revival ministry has been prayer and the preaching of the solid word of God and the naked cross of Jesus Christ.

Over the years, we have witnessed countless lives transformed through the power of God's Word and the fellowship of believers. Our congregation has grown not just in numbers but in spiritual depth and community impact.

Our Leadership

Bishop Ayisi

Lead Pastor

Bishop Ayisi is the visionary founder and lead pastor of Wells of Revival Church. With over 35 years in ministry, Bishop Ayisi is known for his passionate preaching, prophetic insight, and heart for community transformation.

Born and raised in Eastlands Nairobi Kenya, Bishop Ayisi received his call to ministry at a young age.He began his ministry journey that eventually led to the founding of Wells of Revival.

Under his leadership, the church has grown from a small prayer meeting to a thriving congregation that impacts not only Thika but reaches beyond through various outreach programs and missions.

Bishop Ayisi is married to Rev Joyce Ayisis and they have five children..

"My vision is to see a spiritual revival that transforms individuals, families, and communities through the power of God's Word and the demonstration of His love."

What We Believe

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and our final authority for faith and practice.

God

We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Jesus Christ

We believe in the deity of Jesus Christ, His virgin birth, sinless life, atoning death, bodily resurrection, and His coming return.

Salvation

We believe salvation comes through faith in Jesus Christ alone, by grace, not by works.

Holy Spirit

We believe in the present ministry of the Holy Spirit, who indwells believers and empowers them for godly living and service.

The Church

We believe the Church is the Body of Christ, comprising all believers, called to worship, fellowship, and service.
