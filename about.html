Our History & Mission

Wells of Revival Church was founded in [Year] by Bishop Ayisi with a vision to bring spiritual revival to Thika and beyond. What started as a small prayer group in Majengo has grown into a vibrant community of believers dedicated to spreading the Gospel and serving others.

Our name, "Wells of Revival," is inspired by the biblical concept of wells as sources of life-giving water in desert places. Just as ancient wells provided essential nourishment, our church aims to be a source of spiritual refreshment and renewal in our community.

Over the years, we have witnessed countless lives transformed through the power of God's Word and the fellowship of believers. Our congregation has grown not just in numbers but in spiritual depth and community impact.

What We Believe

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and our final authority for faith and practice.

God

We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Jesus Christ

We believe in the deity of Jesus Christ, His virgin birth, sinless life, atoning death, bodily resurrection, and His coming return.

Salvation

We believe salvation comes through faith in Jesus Christ alone, by grace, not by works.

Holy Spirit

We believe in the present ministry of the Holy Spirit, who indwells believers and empowers them for godly living and service.

The Church

We believe the Church is the Body of Christ, comprising all believers, called to worship, fellowship, and service.
