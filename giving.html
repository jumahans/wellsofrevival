Giving

Supporting the Kingdom of God

"Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver." - 2 Corinthians 9:7

Why We Give

Act of Worship

Giving is an act of worship that acknowledges God as the source of all blessings and expresses our gratitude to Him.

Kingdom Advancement

Your giving helps advance God's kingdom through evangelism, discipleship, and community outreach programs.

Church Operations

Your faithful giving supports the day-to-day operations of the church, including facilities, staff, and ministry resources.

Global Missions

Your generosity extends beyond our local community to support missions and humanitarian efforts around the world.

Giving Categories

Tithes

The biblical principle of giving 10% of your income to support God's work through the local church.

Offerings

Gifts given above and beyond tithes to support the general operations and ministries of the church.

Seeds of Faith

Special offerings given in faith for specific breakthroughs or blessings in your life.

Benevolence

Gifts that help those in need within our church and community through our compassion ministries.

Missions

Support for ours mission efforts to spread the Gospel and serve communities.

Financial Accountability

At Wells of Revival Church, we are committed to financial integrity and transparency. We take the stewardship of your giving seriously and ensure that all funds are used effectively for Kingdom purposes.

Our financial practices include:

  • Regular financial audits by independent accountants
  • Oversight by a qualified finance committee
  • Transparent reporting to the congregation
  • Adherence to biblical principles of stewardship

Annual financial reports are available to church members upon request.
